🔥 Advanced AWS Interview Questions (Real-World + Scenario-Based)

If you already know AWS basics, interviews will focus on how you design, secure, and troubleshoot real systems 👇

Here are some advanced AWS questions frequently asked in DevOps/SRE interviews 👇

---

☁️ Architecture & Design

🔹 How do you design a highly available and fault-tolerant architecture in AWS?
🔹 What is the difference between Multi-AZ vs Multi-Region?
🔹 How do you design a scalable system for high traffic applications?
🔹 What are stateless vs stateful architectures in cloud?

---

🌐 Networking (Very Important)

🔹 Explain VPC architecture in detail
🔹 Difference between Security Groups vs NACLs
🔹 How does internet gateway vs NAT gateway work?
🔹 What is VPC Peering vs Transit Gateway (real use case)?
🔹 How does routing work inside a VPC?

---

⚙️ Compute & Scaling

🔹 Difference between Auto Scaling Group vs Load Balancer
🔹 How does Auto Scaling decide when to scale?
🔹 What are launch templates vs launch configurations?
🔹 How do you handle zero downtime deployments in EC2-based apps?

---

💾 Storage

🔹 Difference between EBS vs EFS vs S3
🔹 What are different S3 storage classes and use cases?
🔹 How does S3 versioning & lifecycle policies work?
🔹 What is data durability in S3 (11 9’s)?

---

🔐 Security (Highly Important)

🔹 How does IAM work internally?
🔹 Difference between roles vs policies vs users
🔹 What is least privilege principle?
🔹 How do you manage secrets securely in AWS?

---

🌍 DNS & Traffic Management

🔹 Explain Route 53 routing policies (weighted, latency, failover)
🔹 How does failover routing work in real scenarios?
🔹 How do you achieve blue/green deployment using Route 53?

---

📊 Monitoring & Logging

🔹 Difference between CloudWatch metrics vs logs
🔹 How do you set up alerts for production systems?
🔹 How do you debug high latency or downtime in AWS apps?

---

💰 Cost Optimization (Interview Favorite)

🔹 How do you reduce AWS cost in production?
🔹 What are Reserved Instances vs Spot Instances?
🔹 How do you identify unused resources?

---

🚨 Troubleshooting / Scenarios

🔹 EC2 instance is not reachable — how do you debug?
🔹 Application is slow — what AWS components will you check?
🔹 High bill suddenly — how do you investigate?
🔹 Load balancer is up but traffic not reaching instances — why?

---

💡 Pro Tip:
AWS interviews are less about services and more about:
👉 “How do you design + debug + optimize systems?”

---

If you’re preparing for DevOps/SRE roles, these are must-know 🚀
